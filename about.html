🚀 About Us

We are specialized ICT Technicians who thrive at the intersection of technical logic and creative design. Our expertise ranges from deep-level hardware maintenance and network security to building full-stack mobile applications and websites.

Under our brand Dan74tech Media (or dantyz Media), We coordinate digital communications, design professional branding, edit high-quality video content for musical and corporate projects and build professional user friendly applications and websites.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between complex technology and user-friendly digital experiences through innovative software development and creative media solutions.

Our Vision

To become a leading tech hub in Kenya, empowering businesses with robust IT infrastructure and a modern digital presence that speaks excellence.

🛠️ Why Work With Us?

Technical Precision

We don't just fix problems; we engineer long-term solutions. From database architecture to network persistence, we ensure every detail is optimized.

Creative Strategy

Technology needs to look good. We blend ICT expertise with high-end media production to give your brand a professional, "live-ready" look.

Local Commitment

Based in Meru, we are dedicated to supporting the local community and student organizations through digital literacy and professional branding.
